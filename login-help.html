Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the Login page and click "Forgot Password?"
  2. Enter your registered email address or username
  3. Check your email (including spam/junk folder) for a reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, contact our support team via WhatsApp.

I'm entering the correct credentials but still can't login

This can happen due to several reasons:

  • Caps Lock: Make sure Caps Lock is not enabled — passwords are case-sensitive
  • Browser cache: Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again
  • Multiple accounts: Ensure you're using the correct username for this specific account
  • Account locked: After 5 failed attempts, your account may be temporarily locked for 30 minutes

Still stuck? Reach out to our 24×7 support team and we'll get you back in.

I'm not receiving the OTP code

If you're having trouble receiving OTP codes:

  • Check your mobile signal — OTPs require active network connectivity
  • Wait at least 60 seconds before requesting a new OTP
  • Ensure your registered phone number is correct and active
  • Check if your phone has any SMS blocking apps or DND enabled
  • Try switching to email verification if available

If the problem persists, contact support with your registered number and we'll verify manually.

How do I recover my locked/suspended account?

If your account has been locked or suspended:

  1. Temporary lock: Wait 30 minutes and try logging in again. This resolves most cases.
  2. Security hold: If you see a security warning, contact support with your account details and valid ID for verification.
  3. Policy violation: Accounts suspended for policy violations will receive an email explaining the reason. You can appeal by contacting our support team.

For all account recovery requests, please contact us via WhatsApp or email support@playzone9.co.in.
